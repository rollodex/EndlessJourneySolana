About

Endless Journey is an AI-powered choose your own adventure game. It is based on the concept of a journal, where each choice affects the next entry. Internally, the game uses sophisticated GPT-3 artificial intelligence provided by OpenAI and a specialized prompt designed to generate the next part of the journal and infer/classify the emotion of your character at once. To play, you can either choose the starting prompt or add your own entry to start the story.

How to play

The game works best when prompts are written in first-person, i.e. saying "I" instead of "you". You can either use the default prompt, or create a prompt with an initial story and two branch choices. Try to come up with an emotion and two related adjectives that match the mood of your story. Sometimes you will receive achivements related to the story and not every date will have an achievement. These badges will be minted as an NFT and sent to your Solana address. Try to create cool acheivements to share with your friends!
